CampusAI Sample Knowledge Base

Admissions:
- B.Tech eligibility requires completion of 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
- Required documents include marksheets, transfer certificate, ID proof, passport-size photos, and entrance exam scorecard.

Academics:
- A semester requires 20 credits for full-time registration.
- Elective registration opens in the first two weeks of each semester through the academic portal.

Financial:
- Semester fee payment deadline is 30 days from semester start.
- Merit scholarships are available for top-performing students.

Campus:
- The central library is located near the main academic block.
- Hostel quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM.

Support:
- Students can book counseling through the student support office or the online portal.
