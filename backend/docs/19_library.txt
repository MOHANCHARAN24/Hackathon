Vision
To evolve into a centre of excellence in Science & Technology through creative and innovative practices in teaching - learning, towards promoting academic achievement and research excellence to produce internationally accepted, competitive and world class professionals who are psychologically strong & emotionally balanced imbued with social consciousness & ethical values.
Mission
To provide high quality academic programmes, training activities, research facilities and opportunities supported by continuous industry - institute interaction aimed at promoting employability, entrepreneurship, leadership and research aptitude among students and contribute to the economic and technological development of the region, state and nation.

NTR Vignan Library
The Central library at Vignan's University, inaugurated in the year of 2005 by the Coal and Mines Minister, Govt. of India, Sri. Dasari Narayana Rao has an exclusive user friendly and comfort oriented infrastructure, which is spread over a space of about 5902 Sqm. It is an air conditioned, aesthetically designed facility with plenty of indoor greenery, modern furniture and reprographic conveniences. There are separate enclaves for individual and group study. Students and staff can avail the library from 7.00 AM to 10.00 PM everyday and refer to a wide variety of resources, apart from the books relevant to their curriculum, like a rich collection of titles and journals useful for general study and research activities. The NTR library stocks more than 1,16,114 volumes, which include competitive exam resources for UPSC, IES etc, and plenty of audio-visual learning materials. Also, 261 national and international referred journals are available.

NTR Vignan Library Timings
NTR Memorial Library is kept open on 325 days in a year with the following timings:
Working Days : 7.00 AM - 10.00 PM
Issue Timings : 8.00 AM - 6.00 PM
Holidays : 10.00 AM - 5.00 PM
Changes in Timings during the vacation period will be informed on the Library Notice Board and through circular.
